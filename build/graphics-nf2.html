Navy Field 2 Brand & Positioning Guide

A forthcoming addition to the Nexon game lineup, my team was entrusted with creating a brand and positioning guide for Navy Field 2. We conducted user interviews, feedback sessions, with constant iteration and communication with stakeholders to produce a look for a game that is unique, memorable, and easy to comprehend. My duties included project scoping, copywriting, and presentations to stakeholders.
