Stylus Presentation

CSS Pre-processors are amazing, and I had the honor and privelege to present Stylus to the HTML5 LA Developer's group in Santa Monica. I followed an incredible presentation by Braden Roseborough, UI Engineer at Lynda.com, on Sass and LESS. Over 170 attended, and I spoke about the benefits of using Stylus over other pre-processors (I see it as the hipster of pre-processors. Everything else is just too mainstream). Checkout the Meetup event page for more details, or download my deck below.
