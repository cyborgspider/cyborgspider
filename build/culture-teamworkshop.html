Team Workshop

Always seeking to inspire and to be inspired by others, I ran a creative workshop at work amongst my design colleagues. We brainstormed self-branding and personal logos/identities. After answering some questions about our goals and strengths, people sketched out some ideas, and then we switched the answers among our peers, and had them create one for us! I enjoy facilitating workshops filled with laughs aplenty and creativity abound.
